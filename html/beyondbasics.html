Beyond the Basics

Below are a few additional tags that are useful to know but do not fall within the scope of this particular project.

Aside

The aside tags (<aside> and </aside>) are used for pieces of content that may not relate to the given discussion. They are used much like sidebars are used in print documents (and as we've used them for extra information in this tutorial), though you do not have to present asides as sidebars.
